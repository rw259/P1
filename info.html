What is E-Sports?
Electronic Sports

Electronic Sports are a form of sport competition that use video games as the sport. It has exploded recently with platforms such as Twitch's livestreaming service becoming popular.
This new sporting event is growing, and as more people play video games, there are a small percentage who become extremely good at their respective game.
Now this does not mean that every videogame can become an e-sport, usuallly games that have viability in e-sports have some sort of team based gameplay involved with them.
